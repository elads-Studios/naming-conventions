# Self documenting names

Every name should self document themselves to avoid writing redundant documentation for them.

## Examples

### ✔️ Good

- ✔️ HeadPlayerCharacterEntityMesh
- ✔️ GetIndexFromPosition
- ✔️ maximalHorizontalSpeed

### ❌ Bad

- ❌ Head
- ❌ Get
- ❌ xSpeed
