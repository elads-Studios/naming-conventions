# snake_case

Snake case or snake_case is all in lowercase, where whitespaces are repleaced with single underscores.

## Use case

snake_case is for example used for local variables.

## Examples

### ✔️ Good

- ✔️ entity\_velocity
- ✔️ grid\_index

### ❌ Bad

- ❌ entity\_\_elocity
- ❌ entityVelocity
- ❌ entity\_Velocity
- ❌ Entity\_velocity
- ❌ \_entity\_velocity
- ❌ entity_velocity\_
